# CRM Module

Prospects, leads, activities, campaigns, and contact management.

---

## Pages

### Prospects
- Potential customers not yet converted
- **Features:**
  - Prospect details: company name, contact person, phone, email, source
  - Prospect status: New, Contacted, Qualified, Unqualified, Converted
  - Convert prospect to customer (one-click, carry over data)
  - Prospect scoring (manual or AI-assisted)
  - Source tracking (web, referral, cold call, event, advertisement)
  - Notes and activity history

### Leads / Opportunities
- Sales pipeline tracking
- **Features:**
  - Lead details: prospect/customer, product interest, estimated value, probability
  - Pipeline stages: New, Qualification, Proposal, Negotiation, Won, Lost
  - Expected close date
  - Kanban board view (drag between stages)
  - List view with filters
  - Win/loss reason codes
  - Competitor tracking
  - Document attachments (proposals, contracts)
  - Assign to salesperson

### Activities
- Task and interaction tracking
- **Features:**
  - Activity types: Call, Email, Meeting, Task, Note
  - Link to prospect, customer, lead, or contact
  - Due date, priority, assigned to
  - Activity status: Planned, Completed, Cancelled
  - Calendar view of activities
  - Follow-up reminders
  - Log call outcomes and meeting notes

### Campaigns
- Marketing campaign management
- **Features:**
  - Campaign details: name, type (email, event, social, direct mail), budget, dates
  - Target audience selection (by customer group, region, tags)
  - Campaign status: Planning, Active, Paused, Completed
  - Track responses and conversions
  - ROI calculation (spend vs revenue from campaign leads)
  - Integration with email templates for email campaigns

### Email Templates
- Reusable email templates for CRM communications
- **Features:**
  - Template builder with variable placeholders
  - HTML and plain text versions
  - Template categories (follow-up, introduction, proposal, thank you)
  - Preview with sample data

### Contacts
- Contact directory across all prospects and customers
- **Features:**
  - Contact details: name, title, email, phone, mobile
  - Link to prospect or customer (or standalone)
  - Contact role (decision maker, influencer, technical, billing)
  - Communication preferences
  - Duplicate detection

## Settings

- Lead pipeline stages (customisable names and order)
- Activity types
- Lead sources
- Win/loss reason codes
- Prospect scoring rules
- Campaign types
- Email sending configuration (SMTP, sender address)
- Number series (leads, activities, campaigns)
- Default assigned salesperson rules
