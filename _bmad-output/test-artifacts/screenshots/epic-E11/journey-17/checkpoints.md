# Journey 17: Task Panel Completed/Cancelled Section Toggle — Visual Checkpoints

## Checkpoint 1: Invoice Detail Page with TaskPanel
- **When**: After navigating to invoice detail page (step 2)
- **Screenshot file**: step-2-invoice-detail-with-task-panel.png
- **What to look for**: Invoice detail page loaded, TaskPanel card visible with CheckSquare icon, "Tasks (N)" header, "+ Add Task" button. Card has 12px radius, purple hover shadow styling.

## Checkpoint 2: Completed Toggle Visible (Collapsed)
- **When**: After verifying the completed toggle button exists (step 3)
- **Screenshot file**: step-3-completed-toggle-collapsed.png
- **What to look for**: "Completed (N)" toggle button visible below active tasks, ChevronDown icon indicating collapsed state, uppercase text styling, muted foreground color.

## Checkpoint 3: Completed Section Expanded
- **When**: After clicking the completed toggle to expand (step 4)
- **Screenshot file**: step-4-completed-section-expanded.png
- **What to look for**: Toggle now shows ChevronUp icon, completed tasks visible below with line-through titles, green CheckCircle2 or grey XCircle status icons, muted text styling. Tasks rendered as TaskPanelItem components.
