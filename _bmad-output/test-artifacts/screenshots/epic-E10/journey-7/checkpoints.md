# Visual Checkpoints — Journey 7: Send Document Email Successfully

## Checkpoint 1: Email composition dialog opened
- **When**: After step 3 — opened email dialog via More Actions → Email to Customer
- **Screenshot file**: `step-3-email-dialog-opened.png`
- **What to look for**: Email composition dialog visible as centered modal with purple accent top border. Pre-filled From/To/Subject/Body fields. Send Email button (primary purple) and Cancel button visible in footer. PDF attachment card at bottom.

## Checkpoint 2: Send button visible and enabled
- **When**: After step 4 — verifying Send Email button state before clicking
- **Screenshot file**: `step-4-send-button-enabled.png`
- **What to look for**: Send Email button is purple primary button, enabled (not greyed out), in the dialog footer alongside Cancel button.

## Checkpoint 3: Success toast after sending
- **When**: After step 6 — email sent successfully
- **Screenshot file**: `step-6-success-toast-email-sent.png`
- **What to look for**: Green success toast visible with text 'Email queued for delivery'. Email composition dialog should have closed. Invoice detail page visible underneath with no overlay.

## Checkpoint 4: Dialog fully closed
- **When**: After step 7 — verifying dialog closure
- **Screenshot file**: `step-7-dialog-closed-invoice-detail.png`
- **What to look for**: Invoice detail page fully accessible with no dialog overlay. No error toasts. The page should look normal — same as before the dialog was opened.
