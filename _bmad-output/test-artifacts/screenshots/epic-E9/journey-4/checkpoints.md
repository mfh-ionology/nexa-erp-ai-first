# Visual Checkpoint Manifest — Journey 4: Dismiss a Notification

## Checkpoint 1: Notification dropdown open with initial notifications
- **When**: After step 2 — bell clicked, dropdown opened
- **Screenshot file**: `step-2-dropdown-open-initial-notifications.png`
- **What to look for**: Notification popover visible with at least one notification item. Each item shows title, body preview, relative timestamp, and coloured left border. Unread items have blue dot indicators. Note the count of notifications visible for comparison after dismiss.

## Checkpoint 2: Notification dismissed from list
- **When**: After step 4 — dismiss (X) button clicked on a notification
- **Screenshot file**: `step-4-notification-dismissed.png`
- **What to look for**: Notification dropdown still open. The dismissed notification is no longer in the list. Notification count is one fewer than before. Bell icon badge reflects decreased unread count (or badge disappears if count went to 0).
