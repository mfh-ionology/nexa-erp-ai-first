# Visual Checkpoints — Journey 9: Toggle Article Active/Inactive Status

## Checkpoint 1: Articles Tab Loaded
- **When**: After navigating to /ai/admin/knowledge#articles and tab is active (Step 1)
- **Screenshot file**: step-1-articles-tab-loaded.png
- **What to look for**: Knowledge Articles tab active, article cards visible in grid, each card has a Switch toggle, cards show title/content/badges, switches in ON position for active articles

## Checkpoint 2: Article Deactivated
- **When**: After clicking the active toggle switch on the first article card to deactivate (Step 2)
- **Screenshot file**: step-2-article-deactivated.png
- **What to look for**: Switch moved to OFF/unchecked position, article card appears dimmed/muted, optimistic UI update applied, possible toast notification

## Checkpoint 3: Article Reactivated
- **When**: After clicking the same toggle switch again to reactivate (Step 3)
- **Screenshot file**: step-3-article-reactivated.png
- **What to look for**: Switch moved back to ON/checked position, article card restored to normal appearance (full opacity), possible toast notification
