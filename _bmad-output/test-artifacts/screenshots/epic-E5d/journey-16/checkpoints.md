# Visual Checkpoints — Journey 16: Suggested Knowledge — Reject Platform Suggestion

## Checkpoint 1: Suggested Tab Loaded
- **When**: After navigating to `/ai/admin/knowledge#suggested` and activating Suggested tab
- **Screenshot**: `step-1-suggested-tab-loaded.png`
- **What to look for**: Knowledge Management page with Suggested tab active, suggestion cards visible with Accept/Edit & Accept/Reject buttons, category badges on cards, purple background

## Checkpoint 2: Reject Confirmation Dialog
- **When**: After clicking "Reject" on a suggestion card
- **Screenshot**: `step-2-reject-confirmation-dialog.png`
- **What to look for**: AlertDialog overlay visible with title "Reject Suggestion?", description text about rejecting, Cancel and Reject buttons visible, background dimmed

## Checkpoint 3: After Rejection Confirmed
- **When**: After confirming rejection in dialog
- **Screenshot**: `step-3-rejection-confirmed.png`
- **What to look for**: Rejected card removed/sliding out from suggestions list, success toast "Suggestion rejected" visible, remaining suggestion count decreased or empty state shown
