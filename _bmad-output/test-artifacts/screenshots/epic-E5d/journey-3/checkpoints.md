# Visual Checkpoints — Journey 3: Knowledge Articles Tab — Filtering & Search

## Checkpoint 1: Knowledge Page with Filter Bar
- **When**: After navigating to /ai/admin/knowledge (Step 1)
- **Screenshot**: `step-1-knowledge-page-filter-bar.png`
- **What to look for**: Knowledge Management page loaded, Knowledge Articles tab active, filter bar visible with Category, Source, Active/Inactive toggle, and search input. All filters in default/unselected state.

## Checkpoint 2: Category Filter Popover Open
- **When**: After clicking Category filter button (Step 2)
- **Screenshot**: `step-2-category-popover-open.png`
- **What to look for**: Popover dropdown visible with 5 category checkboxes: Business Processes, Terminology, Industry Rules, Custom Fields, Historical Patterns. None checked by default.

## Checkpoint 3: Terminology Filter Applied
- **When**: After selecting TERMINOLOGY checkbox (Step 3)
- **Screenshot**: `step-3-terminology-filter-applied.png`
- **What to look for**: Only Terminology articles visible in the list. Category filter button shows active state (purple-tinted border/bg). Other category groups hidden.

## Checkpoint 4: Source Filter Popover Open
- **When**: After clicking Source filter button (Step 4)
- **Screenshot**: `step-4-source-popover-open.png`
- **What to look for**: Source popover visible with options: Admin, AI Generated, Platform, From Corrections. Each with color-coded badge.

## Checkpoint 5: Combined Category + Source Filters
- **When**: After selecting AI Generated source (Step 5)
- **Screenshot**: `step-5-combined-filters.png`
- **What to look for**: Articles narrowed to TERMINOLOGY + AI Generated only. Both category and source filter buttons show active state. Article count reduced.

## Checkpoint 6: Inactive Toggle Selected
- **When**: After clicking Inactive toggle (Step 6)
- **Screenshot**: `step-6-inactive-toggle.png`
- **What to look for**: "inactive" toggle button shows selected/active styling. Articles list may be empty or show only inactive articles matching other filters.

## Checkpoint 7: VAT Search Applied
- **When**: After typing "VAT" in search input (Step 7)
- **Screenshot**: `step-7-vat-search.png`
- **What to look for**: Search input contains "VAT". Only articles with "VAT" in title visible. Combined with other active filters.

## Checkpoint 8: All Filters Cleared
- **When**: After clicking Clear filters button (Step 8)
- **Screenshot**: `step-8-filters-cleared.png`
- **What to look for**: All filters reset to defaults. Full article list restored. No active filter indicators. Search input empty. "all" toggle selected.
