# Visual Checkpoints — Journey 2: Knowledge Articles Tab — List View & Category Grouping

## Checkpoint 1: Articles Tab Initial Load
- **When**: After navigating to /ai/admin/knowledge (step 1)
- **Screenshot file**: step-1-articles-tab-loaded.png
- **What to look for**: Knowledge Articles tab is active/selected, page header visible, stats panel with 4 KPI cards above tabs, purple background (#f4f2ff)

## Checkpoint 2: Category Accordion Sections
- **When**: After verifying category accordion sections (step 2)
- **Screenshot file**: step-2-category-accordion-sections.png
- **What to look for**: Articles grouped into accordion sections by category (Business Processes, Terminology, Industry Rules, Custom Fields, Historical Patterns). First 3 sections should be expanded by default. Each section header shows category name and article count badge. Article cards visible within expanded sections with 12px radius and custom shadow.

## Checkpoint 3: Article Card Detail
- **When**: After verifying article card badges and indicators (step 3)
- **Screenshot file**: step-3-article-card-detail.png
- **What to look for**: Individual article card showing: title text, source badge (colour-coded: purple for Admin, blue for AI Generated, green for Platform, amber for Corrections), confidence score percentage in JetBrains Mono with coloured dot indicator, chunk count, usage count, isActive toggle switch in top-right, overflow menu (three dots)

## Checkpoint 4: Needs Review Badge
- **When**: After checking for unconfirmed articles (step 4)
- **Screenshot file**: step-4-needs-review-badge.png
- **What to look for**: Unconfirmed articles display an amber "Needs Review" badge alongside their source badge

## Checkpoint 5: Action Bar Buttons
- **When**: After verifying action bar buttons (step 5)
- **Screenshot file**: step-5-action-bar-buttons.png
- **What to look for**: Two action buttons visible: 'Upload Document' (primary purple #7c3aed button) and 'Create Article' (outline/secondary button). Buttons should be in the action bar area of the page.
