# Visual Checkpoints — Journey 6: Edit Knowledge Article

## Checkpoint 1: Knowledge Articles Tab Loaded
- **When**: After navigating to /ai/admin/knowledge#articles and page loads
- **Screenshot**: `step-1-knowledge-articles-loaded.png`
- **What to look for**: Knowledge Management heading visible, Knowledge Articles tab active, at least one article card visible with overflow menu (three-dot) button

## Checkpoint 2: Overflow Menu Open with Edit Option
- **When**: After clicking the overflow (three-dot) menu on an article card
- **Screenshot**: `step-2-overflow-menu-open.png`
- **What to look for**: Dropdown menu visible with Edit, possibly Confirm, and Delete options

## Checkpoint 3: Edit Dialog Open with Pre-filled Data
- **When**: After clicking Edit from the overflow menu
- **Screenshot**: `step-3-edit-dialog-open.png`
- **What to look for**: Edit dialog visible with pre-filled title, content textarea with existing content, category selector showing current category

## Checkpoint 4: Content Updated in Form
- **When**: After modifying the content textarea with new text
- **Screenshot**: `step-4-content-updated.png`
- **What to look for**: Content textarea shows the new updated text about EU suppliers/reverse charge mechanism, title field unchanged

## Checkpoint 5: Article Updated Successfully
- **When**: After clicking Save and dialog closes
- **Screenshot**: `step-5-article-updated.png`
- **What to look for**: Dialog closed, success toast visible with 'updated' message, article card in list reflects updated content preview
